About Us

This is why we exist.

We came together as a whole to make the lives of the every day person simple, by coming to us to repair their small or larger items.
It can be anything from mobile phones, to computers to even your very own car!
Next we also do developing, such as websites, in the future we are hoping to develop apps and games as well!
We are hosting domains as well, effective, friendly and reliable.

Our Work Process.

Explore.

Explore the cheapest of solutions, then initiate a plan on using these solutions to satisfy the end-user.

Design.

Upon selecting the perfect solution/product, we then design accordingly. Customers get to edit as they see fit but they have limited alterations available.

Develop.

Adding functionality and interactive add-ons do make the site user-friendly and fun but it's a bit harder than design so we do this after the designing to adhere to each and every thing our client desires.

Deliver.

Once done, our client can choose whether they would like for us to host their newly created or an existing domain, or we could just give them the details before/after hosting, if they desire for it to be hosted. If hosting is not desired, we will provide you with the files.
